Bio

Hello, I'm Wey Xie. Throughout my life, I've been drawn to challenging pursuits, eager to understand and navigate life's grand complexities. I've walked paths of academia, finance, high-stakes poker where I was counted among the best, and the roller-coaster world of tech entrepreneurship where I succesfully founded and exited my first company. I've also held the title of the world's number one Call of Duty player, a testament to my commitment in high-pressure environments.

While my accomplishments span various fields, they all converge at one point: the relentless pursuit of growth and understanding. My real passion lies in exploring the human condition, our potential, and how we can thrive in our exponentially evolving world.

My journey hasn't been just about achieving personal success; it's about reshaping how I, and hopefully others, understand life itself. It's about making sense of our existence and figuring out how best we can utilize the time we've been given. I invite you to join me as I continue to explore, learn, and share my insights. Here's to seeking truth, challenging norms, and redefining the human experience in this extraordinary world.
