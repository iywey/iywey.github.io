Bio

Wey Xie is a professional poker player, mindset & lifestyle coach, founder, investor, martial artist, and writer. In 2017, he finished his degree in finance and quit his job to pursue poker. A year later, he competed professionally at the highest stakes, while traveling the world. His early success led him to question the purpose of life, to philosophical and spiritual investigation.

Nowadays, his mission is to help people unleash their potential.
