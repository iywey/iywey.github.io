Oneness

April 2023

The most important realization I have made in my life is that everything is one. Where we see separate things, there are none; there is just existence unfolding. Understanding this is enlightenment.

There is no sense in being upset about certain aspects of life if you are happy about others. They’re all interconnected—spatial and temporal manifestations of one web of causes and effects.

For example, you might be upset about the way your parents raised you, but then you also wouldn’t have had the experiences you most cherish. Love some experiences, hate others—it doesn’t make sense; it all belongs together.

Or, you might be dating a girl, and there are some traits about her that you really don’t like but others that you love. She can’t have one without the other. This is how she is. Love her or hate her, but if you love her, love her for all of her.

All this doesn’t mean we can’t participate in shaping the future—it is ours to shape. This also isn't about merely accepting the bad as a part of life. No, it's way deeper than that: this is the realization that there's only one inseperable process, and you are it.
