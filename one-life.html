One Life

July 2023

It’s possible to never realise it, and it’s easy to forget even if you do: we only have this one opportunity to experience being alive.

This limited collection of moments we call our lives is largely influenced by our own choices. So the question is, how do we want to spend our limited time?

With this awareness, my problems all fall into perspective. I see most of them for what they are—insignificant. Most of the fights I have with the people I love don’t matter at all; I’d rather just hug them and tell them that I love them.

This awareness also illuminates the privilege we have: the ability to decide how we want to spend these moments. I can’t help but smile at the absurdity of how I often choose to use this time.

And it is in this understanding that I gain the clarity to discern what truly matters and the courage to let go of what doesn’t. It fills me with with the peace and joy that comes with remembering that life is an opportunity—one that we have the freedom to live in line with our deepest desires and truest selves.
