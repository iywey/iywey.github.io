Peak Mental State

April 2023

You can experience every moment in completely different ways. 

Perception is a skill—we can improve the way we habitually perceive certain events to be more accurate (closer to truth) and more conducive towards creating our desired outcomes (self-affirming instead of limiting).

In peak state you’re more at ease; fears, insecurities, and compulsions fade; you are really engaged with the reality of your life; and in your actions, you channel all your strengths while understanding your limitations. 

When I’m in this state, the meaning of life is no longer a question to be answered but a self-evident reality. An all-pervasive sense of the sublime unquestionably and forever justifies my existence. Life, even if I only get to appreciate it for a brief moment in time, is deeply known to be worth living.
