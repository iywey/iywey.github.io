Life Philosophy

June 2024

My philosophy can be understood through two words: action and integrity.

Action
Thoughts, feelings, and intentions are volatile and fleeting. They have their place, but in the final analysis what matters is the difference I make to the lives of others. I do this through my actions.

Integrity
Happiness does not come from external circumstance. Understanding this, what matters more is who I am. I judge myself by the alignment of my actions to the deepest understanding I have of myself and the world.

Act with integrity.
